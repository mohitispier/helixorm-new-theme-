FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Online Reputation Management (ORM)?
ORM is the practice of monitoring, managing, and improving how your brand appears online across search engines, social media, review platforms, and forums. Helix ORM helps ensure that positive, authentic content defines your brand's digital presence.
How does Helix ORM place brand mentions on Reddit?
We organically integrate your brand into relevant, high-traffic Reddit conversations. Our team identifies the right subreddits and threads, creating authentic mentions that resonate with real users and drive trust.
What is AI Search Visibility Tracking?
It measures how your brand appears in AI-generated search results from tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude. We track your visibility score, benchmark against competitors, and provide daily shift alerts.
How long does it take to see results?
Most clients see noticeable improvements within 30-60 days. However, comprehensive reputation building is an ongoing process, and the best results come from sustained, long-term efforts.
Can you remove negative content from the internet?
We use a combination of direct removal requests and SEO suppression strategies to push negative content down in search results. While we can't guarantee removal of every piece of content, we significantly reduce its visibility.
Do you offer custom pricing for enterprise clients?
Yes! Our Premium Plan can be tailored to enterprise needs. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific reputation management requirements.
