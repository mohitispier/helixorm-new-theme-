Refund Policy

Clear Expectations for Helix ORM Services

At Helix ORM we focus on delivering strategic Online Reputation Management services that actively shape and protect your brand image. Our work involves real time execution across platforms like Reddit, supported by Quora engagement and backlink building. Because these services require manual effort, content creation and platform interaction this refund policy is designed to set clear expectations while remaining fair to both parties.

01

Service Nature & Refund Eligibility

Helix ORM operates on a performance driven and execution based model. Once a project is initiated our team begins research, planning and content development tailored specifically to your brand. Since this process consumes time and resources from the start and refund eligibility depends on the stage of service.

  • Before work begins
  • Duplicate payments
  • Service not started on time
  • Not Eligible: Work already started, Mentions or reviews posted, Content creation in progress, Delays caused by client
02

Platform Based Refund Terms

Our services are distributed across multiple platforms each with its own workflow and level of effort. Refunds are assessed based on how far the execution has progressed on each platform.

  • Reddit: No refund after posting, Refund before work starts, Partial refund if incomplete
  • Quora: Refund before posting, No refund after submission
  • Backlinks: No refund after publishing, Partial refund before posting
03

Monthly Plans

Helix ORM offers flexible monthly plans designed to provide ongoing reputation management and growth. Each billing cycle represents a fresh set of deliverables and continuous work.

  • No refund after billing starts
  • Cancel before next cycle
  • Unused work may roll over
04

Pay-As-You-Use Services

For clients who choose individual services like comments, reviews or mentions but execution typically begins shortly after purchase. These services are handled as standalone tasks.

  • Refund before execution
  • No refund after start
05

Performance Disclaimer

While we focus on improving your brand reputation, search presence, and trust signals but results may vary depending on multiple external factors beyond our control.

  • No guarantee on rankings
  • No refund for slow results
  • External factors apply
06

Client Responsibility

We need your help to make sure everything goes perfectly and on time. As a client of our company you will work with our team during the process. This means you will have to give client information and approvals to our team when our team needs them from you.

  • Provide details
  • This helps us do our job properly
  • We rely on you to give us the information
  • So we can deliver what you need on time
07

Partial Refunds

In some cases, if work has only been partially completed, we may offer a fair and proportionate refund based on the remaining scope.

  • Incomplete Reddit work
  • Partial Quora or backlinks
  • Scope reduced early
08

Refund Process

We have a simple and transparent refund request process to ensure quick resolution of genuine concerns.

  • Email support
  • Mention reason
  • Apply within 7 days
  • Response time: 3–5 business days
09

Non Refundable Cases

Certain situations do not qualify for refunds especially where services have already been delivered or influenced by external factors.

  • Change of mind
  • Platform issues
  • Scope misunderstandings
  • Work already delivered
10

Cancellation Policy

Clients may choose to cancel services at any time but cancellations must be made before the next billing cycle to avoid charges.

  • Cancel anytime before renewal
  • No mid cycle refunds
  • Active work continues until cycle ends
11

Service Modification Policy

Clients may request changes to their service scope but modifications depend on the stage of execution.

  • Changes allowed before execution
  • Limited changes after start
  • Extra work may incur charges
12

Delivery & Timeline Policy

The timelines we give you are guesses based on how we usually do things. Sometimes things take time because we have to wait for people to approve stuff or check the content or because something outside of our control happens.

  • Timelines are not guaranteed
  • Platform delays not refundable
  • Complex cases may take longer
13

Third Party Platform Disclaimer

We operate across third party platforms like Reddit, Quora and content publishing sites. We do not control their policies, moderation systems or algorithm changes.

  • No control over content removal
  • No guarantee of permanence
  • Platform rules may impact results
14

Abuse & Misuse Policy

We have the right to say no to our services or stop them at any time without giving you your money if we find out that you are not using them in a good way or if you are doing something that is not fair.

  • No spam or illegal activity
  • No misleading brand claims
  • Violation leads to termination
15

Policy Updates

We will update this refund policy sometimes to show changes in our services or how we work. Clients should check it now and then to stay informed about any changes.
