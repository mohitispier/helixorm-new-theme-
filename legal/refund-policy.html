Refund Policy

Helix ORM provides strategic reputation management. Our policy ensures clear expectations for our manual, real-time execution services.

1. Service Nature & Refund Eligibility

Helix ORM operates on a performance-driven and execution-based model. Once initiated, our team begins tailored research and content development.

✅ Eligible for Refund

  • Before work begins
  • Duplicate payments
  • Service not started on time

❌ Not Eligible

  • Work already started
  • Mentions or reviews posted
  • Content creation in progress
  • Delays caused by client

2. Platform Based Terms

Reddit & Quora

Backlinks & Content

Monthly & Pay-As-You-Use Plans

Monthly plans can be cancelled before the next cycle starts. Pay-As-You-Use services (comments, mentions) are refundable only before execution begins.

Apply via email to support within 7 days. Mention your specific reason. Response time is typically 3–5 business days. Cancellations must be made before the next billing renewal.
